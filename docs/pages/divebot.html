Service & Booking
1. How quickly will DriveBot find me a test?
⌄

It depends on your chosen date range and test centres. In most cases, it takes from a few days to a few weeks. The wider your date range and the more test centres you select, the higher the chances of securing an earlier appointment.

2. How do I know if my appointment has been booked successfully?
⌄

You will receive notifications via SMS and voice call once the appointment is successfully booked. You can also check the booking status in your dashboard. Additionally, you'll receive a confirmation email from DVSA.

3. Can I book if I already have a test appointment?
⌄

Yes! Whether you already have a test slot or not, our bot can book one for you. You can opt for the auto-booking service to find an earlier date.

4. Can I choose a specific time slot for my test?
⌄

Due to limited availability of appointments, you can only choose the date, not the specific time slot. The bot will book the first available slot on your preferred dates.

5. How long is my subscription valid?
⌄

Notification Service: 90-day subscription

Auto-Booking Service: Valid for 90 days. If no slot is booked, you can request an extension. The bot will not change your test date after a successful booking without your permission.

6. How do I request a refund?
⌄

Auto-booking users can request a refund through the Subscription page if no slot is booked after 7 days. Notification users can cancel within 48 hours of purchase.

7. What if I need to change my test preferences?
⌄

You can update your test preferences anytime through your Profile page. Changes take effect immediately and our bot will search based on your new criteria.

8. What if the bot can't find me a slot?
⌄

Due to limited availability and competition from other users and software, we cannot guarantee the bot will secure a slot on each attempt. However, the bot will continue trying 24/7 until it successfully books your appointment.

9. How will I receive notifications?
⌄

Notification Service: Must be received via Telegram

Auto-Booking Service: You can join Telegram for real-time updates, or rely on SMS and voice calls after successful booking. Please ensure your contact details are correct.

DVSA Regulations & Rules
10. What are the DVSA rules for changing test dates?
⌄

Free changes: You can change your test date for free if it's more than 10 days away.

Important: Changes within 10 days of the test will result in loss of exam fees. We recommend booking an unsatisfactory slot far in advance, then letting our bot find a better one.

Modification limit: After booking, you can make up to 6 free modifications. Exceeding this limit will result in loss of exam fees.

11. Where can I access the official DVSA website?
⌄

Change existing test: https://www.gov.uk/change-driving-test

Book first test: https://driverpracticaltest.dvsa.gov.uk/application

13. I joined Telegram but don't receive notifications. Why?
⌄

This usually means there are no available test slots within your preferred date range or test centres. Try expanding your date range or adding more test centres to increase your chances.

14. I'm getting an error message. What should I do?
⌄

First, log in to the DVSA website to verify your credentials work correctly. If issues persist, contact our support at info@drivebot.co.uk with your subscription ID and error details.

15. How do I contact customer support?
⌄

Email us at info@drivebot.co.uk. To help us assist you faster, please include your subscription ID and a detailed description of your issue. Due to high volume, we respond within one business day.
